Our Story

Tradition Served Every Day

Aghraharam Filter Coffee was born from a simple dream — to preserve the authentic taste of South Indian tiffin and traditional Filter Coffee, while serving every guest with warmth and hospitality.

The name "Aghraharam" represents heritage, simplicity, community, and timeless traditions. Those values continue to inspire everything we do.

From our first outlet in Guruvayur to our growing journey, our focus has remained the same — serve honest food, use quality ingredients, respect tradition, and delight every customer.
